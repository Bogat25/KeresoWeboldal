What is this game about?

This is a board game that can be played by 3-8 players. Each player is assigned a personality, and they must decide who among them is the most suitable to survive. Due to the threat of overpopulation, the world's leaders have realized that only a certain percentage of the population can survive. Therefore, they have confined people to a room where they must occasionally vote someone out from among themselves. Thus, only those who can convince the others that they deserve to live will remain. Overall, this is a political game where the luck factor of personality generation and the players' persuasive abilities determine the winner.
